Campus

Ambassador

Pravaah, IIT Bhubaneswar's annual Cult & Technopreneurship Fest, invites enthusiastic students to join its Campus Ambassador Program. As a CA, you will represent Pravaah in your college, promote events, build engagement, and help spread the fest's spirit.This role offers leadership experience, certificates, rewards, exclusive perks, and the chance to be part of one of the most vibrant fests in the country.

If you're energetic, creative, and eager to contribute, the Pravaah Campus Ambassador Program is the perfect opportunity for you.

ABOUT PRAVAAH

Pravaah is the annual cultural & technopreneurship festival of IIT Bhubaneswar, celebrating the convergence of culture, innovation, technology, and entrepreneurship. It's a multi-day flagship event that features a mix of competitions, workshops, performances, talks, exhibitions, and networking opportunities, designed to inspire creativity, showcase talent, and foster new ideas.

Pravaah's theme and activities change each year, but its core aim is to ignite passion,encourage collaboration, and create memorable experiences for students.

YOUR ROLES

Be the Face of the Event

You will serve as the representative of the event, answering queries from other students and providing information about the event and its activities

Social Media

As a campus ambassador, your primary role is to spread awareness about the event Pravaah'26 ammong your peers and other students in your network

Publicity

You will be responsible for promoting the event through various channels, such as social-media, posters and personal interactions

Represent Your College

As a campus ambassador, you will represent your college and help to build relationships between Pravaah IIT Bhubaneswar and other institutions
